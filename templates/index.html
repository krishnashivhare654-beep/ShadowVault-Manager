Architecture & Security

ShadowVault is a zero-knowledge password manager. This means your data is encrypted locally using the **AES-256 (Advanced Encryption Standard)** before it even touches the database.

The system generates a unique master.key on your computer. Without this key, the stored data is just a random string of characters (Ciphertext). Even if an attacker gains access to your database file, they cannot retrieve your original passwords.
