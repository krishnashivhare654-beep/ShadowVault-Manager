Technical Details

ShadowVault uses AES-256 Symmetric Encryption. Your data is scrambled into a ciphertext that is impossible to read without the unique master.key generated on your machine.

Built by Krishna Shivare for cybersecurity research.

User Guide
  1. Enter credential details in the "Lock Data" form.
  2. Click "Initialize Encryption" to save.
  3. Your password will appear as a Green Ciphertext in the database.
