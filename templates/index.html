Cryptographic Architecture

ShadowVault uses AES-256 Symmetric Encryption. Your data is scrambled into ciphertext before storage, ensuring that only those with the master.key can read it. Built by Krishna Shivare.

Quick Guide

1. Access the Vault. 2. Enter details. 3. Click Secure & Save. Your password will be stored as an unreadable green cipher.
